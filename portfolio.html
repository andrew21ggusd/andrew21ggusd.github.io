Crystal Defender: Revamp


The Scratch Game in Question

The premise of the game is to shoot monsters and protect your precious crystal.
The scoring system is based on how many mobs or monsters you kill, and the randomness
occurs when the mobs spawn. As time goes down or when a feature is enabled, the mobs
can spawn more frequently and move faster. The game ends when your HP runs out, or time is up.
In certain cases, victoy can be obtained through accumulaltion of points.

After all is said and done, I think that my main inspiration was a game my friend made last year.
This year, I successfully managed to implement two experimental features I thought would be cool last year.
These were big hinderances in my production, but they were ultimately the most succesful points of work with minor chokes here and there.
